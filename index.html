Nicolau Teixeira

My name is Nicolau Teixeira, I am originally from the island of Madeira, Portugal, and I currently live between Portugal, Spain, and France. From an early age, I developed a strong interest in creativity and technology, exploring areas such as drawing, photography, writing, and video games, especially in the mobile space.

I studied Economics, Business Planning, and Data Analysis, and completed a professional training programme in Digital Marketing (2023). That same year, I began actively exploring the field of Artificial Intelligence, applying it to creative and educational projects.

I am also a Bitcoin (BTC) enthusiast and have developed educational projects for beginners and users seeking to deepen their understanding. My work focuses on educating and entertaining people worldwide, without providing financial advice, and is guided by principles of respect, transparency, and responsibility.
